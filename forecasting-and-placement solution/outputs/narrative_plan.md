# Forecasting and Placement Deck Plan

## Audience

Operations sponsor and network planning leads.

## Objective

Show how a weekly category-region forecasting layer can drive better inventory placement decisions, improve fill rate and delivery speed, and reduce reactive transfers.

## Narrative arc

1. Business problem and data reality
2. Forecasting benchmark and chosen policy
3. Placement logic across the fulfillment network
4. KPI impact and quantified trade-offs
5. Recommended actions and limitations

## Slide list

1. Title and decision statement
2. Data grain and modeling approach
3. Forecast benchmark
4. Placement logic and node strategy
5. KPI impact
6. Actions, trade-offs, and limitations
